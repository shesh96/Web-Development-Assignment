Q-1 Why is it called a pseudo-class ?

Ans-A pseudo-class is a selector that selects elements that are in a specific state, e.g. they are the first element of their type, or they are being hovered over by the mouse pointer.

Q-2 What are gradients in CSS?

Ans-CSS defines three types of gradients:

Linear Gradients (goes down/up/left/right/diagonally)
Radial Gradients (defined by their center)
Conic Gradients (rotated around a center point)


Q-3 What are different types of transitions in CSS ?

Ans- CSS transitions provide a way to control animation speed when changing CSS properties.
There are differents types of transitions-
Transition-delay:-The transition-delay property specifies a delay (in seconds) for the transition effect.
Transition-duration:-Specifies how many seconds or milliseconds a transition effect takes to complete.
Transition-property:-Specifies the name of the CSS property the transition effect is for.
Transition-timing-function:-The transition-timing-function property specifies the speed curve of the transition effect.
